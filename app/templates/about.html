About the Algorithm

In understandable terms:
Sports Rankr uses a similar algorithm to chess rankings, or your X-Box to rate and rank current NCAA Division 1A schools.

In data science terms:
Sports Rankr uses a modified Elo/TrueSkill algorithm with a truncated Newton algorithm to optimize input parameters and 9x cross-validation.
This algorithm was trained on each season between 1985 and 2014, and tested on all other seasons. The best input parameters are used for today's rankings.
Schedule strength is the percent above or below the division average of the average opponent rating
